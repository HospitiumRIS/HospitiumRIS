Institution Portal

The Institution Portal is designed for Research Administrators who oversee research activities within academic institutions, hospitals, or research centers.

Dashboard Overview​

The institutional dashboard provides:

  • Researcher Overview - Total researchers, active projects
  • Proposal Summary - Pending reviews, approved, rejected
  • Institutional Metrics - Publication output, grant success rates
  • Recent Activity - Latest submissions and updates

Researcher Management​

Researcher Directory​

View and manage all researchers affiliated with the institution:

ColumnDescription
NameResearcher full name with academic title
DepartmentPrimary department
ORCIDORCID iD (verified badge)
PublicationsTotal publication count
H-IndexResearch impact metric
StatusActive, Inactive, On Leave

Researcher Profiles​

Access detailed researcher profiles including:

  • Complete publication history
  • Active manuscripts and collaborations
  • Submitted proposals
  • Research metrics and trends
  • Professional links and contact information

Filtering & Search​

Find researchers by:

  • Name or email
  • Department
  • Research area
  • Publication count
  • ORCID status

Proposal Review​

Review Queue​

Manage incoming proposals with:

  • Pending Review - New submissions awaiting review
  • Under Review - Currently being evaluated
  • Action Required - Awaiting responses or revisions
  • Completed - Approved or rejected proposals

Proposal Details View​

Review comprehensive proposal information:

  1. Summary Overview

    • Title and abstract
    • Principal Investigator details
    • Co-investigators list
    • Timeline and milestones

  2. Research Details

    • Research objectives
    • Methodology description
    • Expected outcomes

  3. Budget Information

    • Funding source
    • Budget breakdown
    • Cost justification

  4. Ethics Documentation

    • Ethics approval status
    • Consent procedures
    • Data management plans
    • Attached documents

Review Actions​

ActionDescription
ApproveAccept the proposal
RejectDecline with feedback
Request RevisionReturn for modifications
Assign ReviewerDelegate to another admin
Add CommentInternal notes

Best Practices​

Proposal Review​

  1. Review within 5 business days
  2. Provide constructive feedback on rejections
  3. Check ethics documentation completeness
  4. Verify budget reasonableness
  5. Confirm co-investigator availability

Researcher Support​

  1. Respond to queries promptly
  2. Provide onboarding resources
  3. Facilitate inter-departmental collaborations
  4. Share funding opportunities
  5. Recognize research achievements
