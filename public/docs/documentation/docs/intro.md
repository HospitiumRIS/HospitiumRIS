# Welcome to HospitiumRIS

**HospitiumRIS** (Hospitium Research Information System) is a comprehensive, modern web-based platform designed to streamline research management for academic institutions, healthcare organizations, and research foundations.

## What is HospitiumRIS?

HospitiumRIS is an integrated Research Information System that brings together researchers, institutions, and foundations in a unified platform. It facilitates the entire research lifecycle - from proposal creation and manuscript collaboration to publication management and grant tracking.

### Key Highlights

- **Research-Centric Design** - Built specifically for academic and healthcare research environments
- **Collaborative Workflows** - Real-time manuscript collaboration with track changes and version control
- **Comprehensive Analytics** - Research metrics, publication tracking, and impact visualization
- **ORCID Integration** - Seamless researcher identification and profile management
- **Grant & Campaign Management** - Track funding opportunities and manage donation campaigns
- **Role-Based Access Control** - Secure, multi-tenant architecture supporting different user types

## Who is HospitiumRIS For?

### Researchers
Manage your research profile, collaborate on manuscripts, track publications, submit proposals, and monitor your research impact with integrated metrics like H-Index and citation counts.

### Research Administrators
Oversee institutional research activities, manage researcher profiles, review proposals, and access institutional analytics.

### Foundation Administrators
Manage fundraising campaigns, track donations, discover grant opportunities, and oversee foundation-wide research funding.

### Super Administrators
Full system control including user management, database operations, and platform-wide configuration.

## Core Modules

| Module | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Researcher Portal** | Profile management, manuscripts, publications, proposals, and analytics |

## Key Features

### Collaborative Manuscript Editor
A powerful real-time collaborative editor built with TipTap, featuring:
- Track changes with accept/reject workflow
- Version history and restoration
- Inline comments and suggestions
- Citation management with BibTeX support
- Multi-author collaboration with role-based permissions

### Publication Library
- Import publications from PubMed, CrossRef, OpenAlex, and Zotero
- Automatic metadata extraction and deduplication
- Folder-based organization
- Citation tracking and impact metrics

### Proposal Management
- Structured proposal creation with ethical considerations
- Co-investigator management with ORCID lookup
- Milestone and deliverable tracking
- Budget allocation and funding source management
- File attachments for ethics documents and data management plans

### Campaign & Grant Management
- Fundraising campaign creation and tracking
- Donation recording and analytics
- Grant opportunity discovery
- Grantor relationship management

### Research Profile
- ORCID-verified profiles with automatic data sync
- Research interests and specializations
- Academic metrics (H-Index, citations)
- Professional links (Google Scholar, LinkedIn, Twitter)
- Public researcher profiles with shareable URLs

## Technology Stack

HospitiumRIS is built with modern, production-ready technologies:

- **Frontend**: Next.js 16, React 19, Material UI 7
- **Backend**: Next.js API Routes, Prisma ORM
- **Database**: PostgreSQL
- **Rich Text Editor**: TipTap
- **Visualizations**: D3.js, Chart.js, React Force Graph
- **Authentication**: Custom auth with ORCID OAuth integration
